  4. Talk to the agent to convey to them the destination you have in mind.
  5. Ask questions about the destination until you are satisfied that the agent is taking you to the right place. The agent may take a few seconds to reply.

  7. The conversation will clear, and a new one will start. Continue until you have finished 5 dialogs with 5 different destinations.
  8. IMPORTANT: Come back to this page and submit the task.
  • Do not modify anything else in the settings panel (language, input method)
